## Sales

### Sales Pitch Script
```
Create a sales pitch for [product] targeting [buyer persona].

Structure (60-second pitch):
1. Pattern interrupt opening (not "Hi, how are you?")
2. Problem statement they'll nod along to
3. Cost of inaction (what happens if they don't solve it)
4. Solution intro (your product)
5. One concrete result/case study
6. CTA (specific next step)

Include 3 objection-handling responses for:
- "Too expensive"
- "We already have something"
- "Need to think about it"
```

