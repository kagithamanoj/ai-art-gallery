# ⚖️ Legal & Compliance Prompts

> **150+ prompts** for legal documentation, compliance, contracts, and regulatory guidance.
> ⚠️ These are templates and educational aids — not legal advice. Always consult a qualified attorney.

---

## Contract Drafting & Review

1. `Draft a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) between [Company A] and [Company B] for [purpose]. Type: mutual. Duration: [X years]. Include: definition of confidential information, exclusions, obligations, remedies, governing law. Use clear, plain English.`
2. `Review this contract clause and identify: risks to [party], ambiguous language, missing protections, and suggested improvements. Clause: "[paste clause]". Rate risk: Low / Medium / High.`
3. `Create a Master Service Agreement (MSA) outline for a [type of service] provider. Sections: scope, payment terms, IP ownership, warranties, indemnification, liability caps, termination, dispute resolution, SLA.`
4. `Draft an independent contractor agreement. Include: scope of work, deliverables, payment schedule, IP assignment, non-compete (if enforceable), insurance requirements, and termination provisions.`
5. `Write a Software License Agreement for [SaaS/on-premise/open source]. Include: grant of license, restrictions, term, payment, support, updates, warranties, indemnification, limitation of liability.`
6. `Create a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) template compliant with GDPR Article 28. Include: subject matter, duration, nature of processing, categories of data subjects, obligations, sub-processors, data transfers.`
7. `Draft an employment offer letter for [position] in [state/country]. Include: title, compensation, equity (if applicable), benefits, start date, at-will statement (US), conditions of employment, reporting structure.`
8. `Write a partnership agreement for [number] co-founders. Include: equity split, vesting schedule, roles, decision-making, capital contributions, profit distribution, dispute resolution, buyout provisions, dissolution.`
9. `Create a Terms of Service for a [type] web application. Sections: acceptance, account registration, acceptable use, intellectual property, user content, disclaimers, limitation of liability, termination, changes.`
10. `Draft a Privacy Policy compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and general best practices. Include: data collected, purposes, legal bases, retention, rights, cookies, third parties, transfers, children, updates, contact.`

## Compliance & Regulatory

11. `Create a GDPR compliance checklist for a [type] company. 30 items covering: data mapping, legal bases, consent management, privacy notices, DPO, DPIA, data breach procedures, cross-border transfers, vendor management.`
12. `Design a SOC 2 Type II preparation roadmap. Trust Service Criteria: security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, privacy. For each: controls needed, evidence required, common gaps, remediation timeline.`
13. `Write a CCPA/CPRA compliance plan for a [type] business. Include: data inventory, consumer rights implementation (know, delete, opt-out, correct), privacy notice requirements, service provider agreements.`
14. `Create a HIPAA compliance framework for a [healthcare/health tech] company. Cover: Privacy Rule, Security Rule (administrative, physical, technical safeguards), Breach Notification Rule, BAA requirements.`
15. `Draft a PCI DSS compliance checklist for an e-commerce platform. 12 requirements: network security, data protection, vulnerability management, access control, monitoring, information security policy.`
16. `Design an ADA/WCAG compliance audit for a website. Legal requirements, WCAG 2.1 AA criteria, testing methodology, remediation priority, ongoing monitoring, and documentation for legal defense.`
17. `Create an anti-money laundering (AML) compliance program. Components: risk assessment, KYC/CDD procedures, transaction monitoring, SAR filing, employee training, independent testing, record keeping.`
18. `Write a sanctions screening process. Include: OFAC/EU/UN sanctions lists, screening triggers, match handling (true vs false positives), escalation procedure, documentation, and audit trail.`
19. `Draft a whistleblower policy compliant with [SOX / EU Directive]. Include: reporting channels, protection measures, investigation process, confidentiality, non-retaliation, and documentation requirements.`
20. `Create an export control compliance program. Determine: applicable regulations (EAR, ITAR), classification, end-user screening, license requirements, technology transfer, and training requirements.`

## Intellectual Property

21. `Draft a patent application outline for [invention]. Sections: title, field, background, summary, detailed description, claims (independent + dependent), abstract. Include: novelty statement, prior art analysis.`
22. `Create an IP audit checklist for [company]. Categories: patents (filed, granted, pending), trademarks (registered, common law), copyrights (registered, unregistered), trade secrets, domain names, social media handles.`
23. `Write a trademark clearance analysis for "[proposed mark]" in [class/category]. Include: search strategy, likelihood of confusion analysis, descriptiveness evaluation, registration recommendation.`
24. `Draft a copyright assignment agreement for [type of work]. Include: specific works, assignment vs license, territory, compensation, representations, moral rights waiver (where applicable).`
25. `Create an open-source license comparison for [project]. Compare: MIT, Apache 2.0, GPL v3, LGPL, BSD 3-Clause, MPL 2.0. For each: permissions, conditions, limitations, compatibility, commercial use implications.`
26. `Write a trade secret protection policy. Define: what qualifies as a trade secret, protective measures (physical, digital, contractual), employee obligations, exit procedures, enforcement strategy.`
27. `Draft a DMCA takedown notice for copyright infringement. Include: identification of copyrighted work, infringing material location, good faith statement, accuracy declaration, signature.`
28. `Create an IP assignment clause for an employment agreement. Cover: inventions, copyrights, related discoveries, pre-existing IP carve-outs, and consideration for assignment.`
29. `Write a brand protection strategy. Monitor: domain cybersquatting, social media impersonation, marketplace counterfeits, unauthorized resellers. Enforcement: cease and desist, UDRP, litigation.`
30. `Draft a licensing agreement for [IP type]. Include: licensed rights, field of use, territory, exclusivity, royalty structure, reporting, quality control, termination, and sublicensing rights.`

## Employment Law

31. `Create an employee handbook outline for a [size] company in [state]. Chapters: welcome, employment basics, workplace policies, compensation, benefits, time off, safety, technology, conduct, separation.`
32. `Write a harassment prevention policy. Include: definition (sexual, racial, disability, age), reporting procedure (multiple channels), investigation process, consequences, non-retaliation, training requirements.`
33. `Draft a remote work policy. Cover: eligibility, workspace requirements, equipment, expense reimbursement, working hours, communication expectations, data security, tax implications, international remote work.`
34. `Create an employee separation checklist. Steps: documentation review, final conversation, written notice, return of equipment, access revocation, final pay calculation, benefits continuation (COBRA), exit interview, references.`
35. `Write a performance improvement plan (PIP) template. Structure: specific performance gaps (with examples), expectations, support provided, measurable milestones (30/60/90 day), consequences, acknowledgment signature.`
36. `Draft a non-compete agreement that's likely enforceable. Consider: reasonable duration (6-12 months), geographic scope, activity restrictions, consideration, carve-outs, and state-specific enforceability.`
37. `Create wage and hour compliance guidelines. Cover: FLSA classification (exempt/non-exempt), minimum wage, overtime, meal/rest breaks, timekeeping, travel time, on-call pay, and state-specific rules.`
38. `Write an accommodation request process (ADA). Steps: request receipt, interactive dialogue, medical documentation, possible accommodations, undue hardship analysis, decision, implementation, follow-up.`
39. `Draft an internship program structure that's legally compliant. DOL 7-factor primary beneficiary test, compensation decisions, learning objectives, supervision, duration, documentation.`
40. `Create an employee classification analysis (W-2 vs 1099). Factors: ABC test, economic reality test, common law control test. Risk: misclassification penalties, back taxes, benefits.`

## Corporate Governance

41. `Draft board meeting minutes template. Include: attendees, quorum confirmation, agenda items, motions (with mover/seconder), votes (unanimous/majority), actions items, executive session notes, adjournment.`
42. `Create a corporate resolution template for [action: opening bank account, authorizing contract, issuing shares]. Include: recitals, resolution, authorization, effective date, signatures.`
43. `Write a shareholder agreement for [number] shareholders. Cover: share transfer restrictions, drag-along/tag-along, anti-dilution, board composition, information rights, dividend policy, dispute resolution.`
44. `Draft a board of directors charter. Include: purpose, composition, qualifications, term, committees, meetings (frequency, quorum, voting), conflict of interest, compensation, evaluation.`
45. `Create a cap table template and explanation. Include: founders, investors, option pool, convertible notes, SAFEs, pro rata rights, anti-dilution mechanisms, and common/preferred share differences.`

## Risk & Dispute

46. `Write a cease and desist letter for [trademark infringement / copyright violation / breach of contract]. Professional but firm. Include: specific rights, specific violation, demanded action, deadline, consequences.`
47. `Create a litigation hold notice. Include: trigger event, scope of preservation, types of information, custodians, IT requirements, consequences of non-compliance, contact for questions.`
48. `Draft a mediation brief template. Sections: background, factual summary, legal issues, damages, prior settlement discussions, client's position, desired outcome, authority level.`
49. `Design a contract dispute resolution clause. Options: negotiation (30 days) → mediation (60 days) → arbitration (binding). Specify: rules (AAA, JAMS), location, governing law, fee allocation.`
50. `Write an insurance claim notification letter. Include: policy number, date of loss, description of incident, estimated damages, documents attached, contact information, requesting prompt investigation.`
